What is Plagiarism?

Plagiarism involves copying someone else's work or ideas without giving proper credit. It can lead to serious consequences, both academically and legally.

Reported Cases of Plagiarism

  • Case 1: A student caught copying research papers from the internet.
  • Case 2: A writer accused of stealing portions of a novel from another author.

Consequences and Punishments

The punishment for plagiarism varies by country:

Country Punishment
USA Fines, suspension, or expulsion from academic institutions
UK Fines, academic disciplinary action
India Legal action, fines, and jail time in severe cases
